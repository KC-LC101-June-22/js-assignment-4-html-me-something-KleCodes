Some of My Favorite Foods

Below is a list of some of my favorites foods with pics!

  • Tacos
  • Mac and Cheese
  • Peanut Butter

Tacos

Tacos are by far my favorite food. I could eat tacos everyday and be just fine lol. Lately, I am loving birria tacos (pictured). The consome makes all the difference in a good birria taco. It needs to be packed with flavor. Be carefuel cooking it though; the vapors from cooking the peppers can be overwhelming.

Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese is definitely a close 2nd when it comes to my favorite foods. I eat it by itself as a meal all the time. Traditional recipes are best for me, but I can sometimes handle it with bbq meat or seafood mixed in.

Peanut Butter

Peanut butter is another favorite of mine. Specifically, I love peanut butter with apples. It is an excellent snack when I'm craving something sweet. My next goal is to try my hand a making peanut butter in the Vitamix.
